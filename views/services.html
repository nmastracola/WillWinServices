Our Services

  • Supervised Parent-time
  • Provides the opportunity for the non-residential parent to spent quality time with his/her child(ren) in a safe, neutral environment.
  • The child(ren) is within sight and hearing of a supervisor.
  • Parent-time can occur on or off-site.
  • Objective documentation
  • Monitored phone visits are also available.
  • Supportive Parent-time
  • Uses a clinically trained supervisor who plays a role in helping child(ren) and parent interact
  • Assists parent in building parenting skills to strengthen the parent/child relationship
  • Neutral Exchanges
  • Offers safety and avoids potential conflict when parents are exchanging children for parent-time.
  • Neutral Exchanges are helpful for parents who have a protective order or a no-contact order.
  • Allows for staggered arrival and depatured times.
  • Parent-to-Parent Conferencing & Mediation
  • Confidential process
  • Parents meet with a mediator and/or program coordinator who facilitates a conversation between parents
  • Parents explore the challenges of parenting and work together to form a plan that provides their child(ren) happy, safe, and conflict free interaction with both parents.
